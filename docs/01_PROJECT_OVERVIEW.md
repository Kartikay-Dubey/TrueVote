# PROJECT OVERVIEW: TrueVote

## The Future of Fair and Open Elections
TrueVote is a cybersecurity-focused, decentralized-style voting application designed to eradicate election fraud and increase voter trust. Our mission is to provide an end-to-end verifiable system where citizens can cast votes securely from their devices and independently verify that their exact choice was counted in the final tally without revealing their identity.

### Problem Statement
Traditional voting systems rely on blind trust. Voters drop a ballot in a box or press a button on a machine, but they have zero mathematical proof that their specific vote was tallied correctly. Furthermore, traditional systems are vulnerable to physical tampering, double-voting, and systemic miscounts.

### The TrueVote Solution
TrueVote solves this by bringing "Bank-Grade Security" to the ballot box. We utilize strict identity gating (JSON Web Tokens) to guarantee one vote per citizen. Once a choice is made, our backend engine encrypts the vote into an immutable SHA-256 cryptographic receipt. The voter walks away with proof of their vote, making the election 100% transparent and 100% anonymous simultaneously.
